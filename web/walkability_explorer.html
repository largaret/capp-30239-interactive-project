National Walkability Index

Each block's NWI score is calculated using the following formula:

score = w Street intersection density 3 + x Distance to nearest transit stop, as a proxy for transit accessibility 3 + y A measure of employment mix, as a proxy for land use diversity 6 + z A measure of employment and household mix 6
where w, x, y, and z are the ranked values (1-20) of each component.
